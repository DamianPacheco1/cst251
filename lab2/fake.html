History of The Polaroid

The Polaroid Corporation was founded by American scientist Edwin H. Land in 1937. The first Polaroid camera, called the Model 95, and its associated film went on sale in 1948 at a department store in Boston. It was revolutionary in that it transformed the photographic medium in ways that can still be seen today.
