What are Digital Image Filters

Digital Image Filters are overlay presets that you can put on top of a digital photo to change the overall look and feel of the photo. You can use these filters on photo editing softwares like Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Lightroom.

History of Digital Image Filters

Digital image editing was made in 1980s from the creation of photoediting programs on computers. Adobe Photoshop was also made during the 80s' and released in 1987 and we still use it a lot in the photography scene. Why Use Digital Image Filters?

Through the use of digital image filters you can do things like blurring the background to bring focus towards a object, make a picture feel older by adding a black and white filter or a grain filter, you can even change the hue of the image to make it more red or green. The possibilitys are endless.
